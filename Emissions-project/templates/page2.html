Notes

Methodology

This is a simplified method of calculating GHG emissions from diesel-powered generators or diesel-powered stationary combustion engines by utilizing emission factors for stationary combustion engines based on fuel (diesel) consumption. Be sure to add the fuel consumption of all generators/engines for the desired time frame (i.e., hour, day, week. month, quarter, year, etc. ) if you have more than one generator/engine.
